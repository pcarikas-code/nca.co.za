# National Credit Act, 2005 (Act 34 of 2005)

**Key Amendments:**
*   **Consumer Protection Act, 2008 (Act 68 of 2008):** Amended on 31 March 2011.
*   **National Credit Amendment Act, 2014 (Act 19 of 2014):** Amended on 13 March 2015.
*   **Financial Sector Regulation Act, 2017 (Act 9 of 2017):** Amended on 1 October 2018.
*   **Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (Act 4 of 2013):** Amended on 30 June 2021.

**Key Provisions & Changes:**
*   **Debt Intervention:** Introduced by the 2014 Amendment Act (often referred to as "debt relief") for low-income consumers.
*   **Affordability Assessment Regulations:** Strengthened requirements for credit providers to verify income and expenses.
*   **Credit Information Amnesty:** Removal of adverse consumer credit information (paid-up judgments).
*   **Registration of Credit Providers:** Lowered thresholds for registration.
*   **Reckless Lending:** Clearer definitions and penalties.
